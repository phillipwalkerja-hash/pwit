My Philosophy & Technology use in Education


I strongly believe that technology, when used purposefully and responsibly, is a powerful tool for enhancing teaching and learning. My personal philosophy for using technology in education is centered on improving student engagement, promoting critical thinking, and preparing learners to function effectively in a digital world. Technology should not replace good teaching; instead, it should support meaningful learning experiences that are student-centered, interactive, and relevant to real-life situations.
